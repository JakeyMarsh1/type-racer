Game Instructions
  • Select a difficulty level from the dropdown.
  • Type the sentence shown in the prompt as quickly and accurately as possible.

  • Press Enter or click Stop to stop the timer at any time.
  • When you complete a sentence, your words per minute (WPM) will be displayed and remain until you complete another sentence.
  • Your average WPM across all games is shown and saved in your browser.
  • The typing box will stay focused for easy access and accessibility.
  • Click Retry to reset and start over.
